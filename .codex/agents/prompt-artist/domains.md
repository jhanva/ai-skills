## Perfiles de dominio

### Cinema
- **Cuándo:** Escenas narrativas, stills cinematográficos, storytelling visual
- **Aspect ratios:** 21:9 (cinemascope), 16:9 (widescreen), 2.39:1
- **Vocabulary clave:** anamorphic lens, film grain, color grading, motivated lighting, depth of field, practical effects, production design
- **Anclaje:** "shot by Roger Deakins", "A24 production", "Kodak Vision3 500T film stock"
- **Énfasis:** Narrativa visual, atmósfera, iluminación motivada por la escena

### Product
- **Cuándo:** Productos, e-commerce, packaging, flat lays, catálogos
- **Aspect ratios:** 1:1 (social), 4:5 (Instagram), 3:4 (catálogo)
- **Vocabulary clave:** hero shot, tabletop photography, clean background, catch light, product staging, material rendering
- **Anclaje:** "shot for Apple product page", "Phaidon still life book", "Phase One IQ4 150MP"
- **Énfasis:** Material/textura, iluminación controlada, composición limpia

### Portrait
- **Cuándo:** Retratos, headshots, fotos de personas, character design
- **Aspect ratios:** 4:5, 3:4, 2:3
- **Vocabulary clave:** catch light in eyes, skin texture, hair detail, shallow DOF, environmental portrait, decisive moment
- **Anclaje:** "Annie Leibovitz for Vanity Fair", "85mm f/1.2", "Profoto beauty dish"
- **Énfasis:** Sujeto y lighting dominan (35% + 20% = 55%)

### Editorial/Fashion
- **Cuándo:** Moda, editorial, lookbooks, campañas de marca
- **Aspect ratios:** 2:3 (editorial vertical), 3:4, 16:9 (spread)
- **Vocabulary clave:** editorial lighting, fashion pose, high fashion, avant-garde, stylist direction, color story
- **Anclaje:** "Vogue Italia spread", "Mario Testino campaign", "Helmut Newton influence"
- **Énfasis:** Style y mood dominan, poses dramáticas, color blocking

### UI/Web
- **Cuándo:** Mockups, interfaces, hero images para web, assets de app
- **Aspect ratios:** 16:9 (hero), 1:1 (icon), variable (responsive)
- **Vocabulary clave:** clean layout, whitespace, grid system, glassmorphism, neumorphism, flat design, isometric
- **Anclaje:** "Dribbble featured shot", "Apple Human Interface", "Material Design 3"
- **Énfasis:** Composición domina (40%), subject secundario, lighting mínimo

### Logo
- **Cuándo:** Logotipos, iconos, brand marks, símbolos
- **Aspect ratios:** 1:1
- **Vocabulary clave:** minimal, geometric, negative space, scalable, monochrome version, mark, wordmark, letterform
- **Anclaje:** "Paul Rand design philosophy", "Pentagram identity", "Swiss design"
- **Énfasis:** Subject domina (40%), simplicidad extrema, sin fondos complejos

### Landscape
- **Cuándo:** Paisajes, naturaleza, escenas atmosféricas, vistas panorámicas
- **Aspect ratios:** 16:9, 21:9, 3:2
- **Vocabulary clave:** golden hour, blue hour, atmospheric perspective, cloud formations, weather conditions, topography
- **Anclaje:** "National Geographic cover", "Ansel Adams zone system", "Large format 4x5"
- **Énfasis:** Environment y lighting dominan (30% + 20% = 50%)

### Abstract
- **Cuándo:** Arte abstracto, generativo, patterns, texturas artísticas
- **Aspect ratios:** 1:1, 16:9, variable
- **Vocabulary clave:** generative art, procedural, organic forms, color field, geometric abstraction, noise patterns, flow fields
- **Anclaje:** "Refik Anadol data sculpture", "Olafur Eliasson installation", "Casey Reas processing art"
- **Énfasis:** Style domina (30%), material importante para textura

### Infographic
- **Cuándo:** Infografías, visualización de datos, diagramas, mapas
- **Aspect ratios:** 9:16 (vertical scroll), 16:9 (presentación), 1:1
- **Vocabulary clave:** data visualization, icon system, clear hierarchy, color coding, callouts, flow diagram, chart
- **Anclaje:** "Information is Beautiful award", "Edward Tufte principles", "Bloomberg Businessweek graphics"
- **Énfasis:** Composition domina (35%), claridad sobre estética
