# Humanize EN Markers

Lee este archivo solo cuando el texto este en ingles o mezclado con ingles.

## Strong vocabulary markers

Replace or rewrite when they sound decorative or generic:

```text
delve, tapestry, landscape as metaphor, multifaceted, nuanced as filler,
pivotal, paramount, cornerstone, foster, leverage as verb, streamline,
underscore, intricate, realm, embark, navigate as metaphor, spearhead,
groundbreaking, transformative, game-changing, cutting-edge, holistic,
synergy, robust outside technical context, seamless, comprehensive as filler,
utilize, facilitate
```

## Formulaic phrases

Remove or restructure when they read like template copy:

```text
It's important to note that...
It's worth mentioning that...
In today's rapidly evolving...
In conclusion...
Overall, ...
Firstly, ... Secondly, ... Thirdly, ...
Whether you're a... or a...
Let's dive in...
Without further ado...
```

## Common grammar and tone signals

- Repeated hedge words without evidence.
- Symmetric paragraph rhythm.
- Balanced-but-empty prose that avoids taking a position.
- Generic enthusiasm without proof.
- Over-clean transitions between every paragraph.

## Structural signals

- Intro repeats the prompt instead of adding value.
- Every section has the same length and shape.
- Closing paragraph only restates prior points.
- Lists are perfectly parallel and oddly polished for the topic.
